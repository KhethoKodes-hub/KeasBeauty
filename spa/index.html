Welcome to Kea's Beauty Studio

Kea's Beauty Studio is a beauty and hair care business owned by a young woman with a passion for beauty, entrepreneurship, and helping others feel confident.

Founded with the vision of providing quality hair services in a welcoming and professional environment, Kea's Beauty Studio aims to empower clients through beauty while continuously growing into a trusted and recognised brand within the industry. Every client who walks through our doors deserves to leave feeling beautiful, confident, and satisfied — and that is exactly what we deliver.
