About Kea's Beauty Studio

Kea's Beauty Studio is a beauty and hair care business owned by a young woman with a passion for beauty, entrepreneurship, and helping others feel confident. The studio was founded with the vision of providing quality hair services in a welcoming and professional environment.

Kea's Beauty Studio aims to empower clients through beauty while continuously growing into a trusted and recognised brand within the industry. Every client who walks through our doors deserves to leave feeling beautiful, confident, and completely satisfied — and that is exactly what we deliver, every single time.

Braiding

Knotless, box and freehand braids crafted with precision and care.

Straight Styles

Sleek straight backs and sharp straight ups for any occasion.

Protective Styles

Beautiful protective hairstyles that promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Washing

Professional hair washing and care to keep your hair healthy.

Our Core Values

Everything we do at Kea's Beauty Studio is rooted in these core values that guide how we serve our clients and grow our brand.

Professionalism

We conduct ourselves with the highest level of professionalism in every service we deliver and every interaction we have with our clients.

Creativity

We bring creativity and artistry to every style, ensuring each client's look is unique, beautiful and a true reflection of their personality.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. From the products we use to the techniques we apply, everything is done to the highest standard possible.

Customer Satisfaction

Our clients' satisfaction is our greatest reward. We go above and beyond to ensure every client leaves happy, confident and looking their best.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in everything we do, building trust with our clients through consistent and ethical conduct.

Consistency

We deliver the same high standard of service every time, so our clients can always count on us to meet their expectations, appointment after appointment.
